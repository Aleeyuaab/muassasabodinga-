ANKAFA WANNAN MAKARANTAR DOMIN ADDININ MUSULUNCI DA KUMA TARBIYA TA GARI

DOKA! DOKA!! DOKA!!!

1. Wajibi ne a biya kudin takardar shiga makaranta (Registration form) ₦ 500, da kuma rigista ₦ 500. Haka kuma
a kowanne zango (wata hudu) za'a biya kudin makaranta ₦ 2000 ga bangaren Islamiyya,
bangaren tahfiz kuma ₦ 3000, Wanda keson hada bangarorin guda biyu zai biya ₦ 5000.

2. Wajibi ne ga duk dalibin da keson shiga wannan makaranta, mahaifinsa ko wakilinsa ya kawo sa.

3. Wajibi ne tufafin makaranta sukance bisa tsarin musulunci kamar haka:-
a. MAZA: Tagguwa (riga), wando da hula. b. MATA: Tagguwa (riga), wando Dankwali da kuma hijabi har kasa.

4. Dole ne Dalibi/Daliba Su rika zuwa makaranta koda yaushe kuma a cikin lokacin da aka tsara, rashin yin haka za'a yi musu hukunci daya dace.

5. Ba'a yarda kowacce daliba tazo makaranta da kwalliya ba.

6. Ba'a yarda dalibi yazo da yawar salula ko wata na'ura ta zamani ba (bluetooth, smartwatch).

7. Idan yaro ko yarinya suka kai kara anyi musu wani abu,to a nemi gaskiyar magana a makaranta.

8. Idan za'a yi tafiya da dalibi ko daliba, to a nemi izini daga makaranta, idan ba haka ba za'a yi musu hukunci idan sun dawo.

9. Idan za'a cazawa dalibi makaranta to wajibine a sanarda hukumar makaranta tare da karbar takardar shedar canjin makaranta (Transfer letter).

10. Lokutan karatu sune kamar haka:- a. ISLAMIYYA: Assabar zuwa laraba karfe 3pm -6pm b. TAHFIZ: Assabar da lahadi karfe 8am -1pm, Laraba 2pm -4pm, Alhamis karfe 3pm -6pm.

11. Wajibi ne dalibi / daliba su mallaki kayan karatu kamar yadda makaranta ta tsara.

12. Wajibi ne ga mahaifi / wakili ya karbi kira idan makaranta ta kira taron uwayen yara ko mitin (meetin).
